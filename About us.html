ABOUT US

Uchi sushi is a brand Vietnamese restaurant that been active for 6 years with 3 restaurants in Ho Chi Minh city.

After an accident that could have led to his separation from the kitchen profession, Chef Ky Cuong overcame the injury to open his own Japanese restaurant- Uchi sushi, and still did not give up his dream of being a top chef, he was in the top 14 of top chefs in Vietnam 2019.

VISION

To be a cornerstone of the Vietnamese
culinary scene, where tradition meets
innovation to create a uniquely
Vietnamese sushi experience.

MISSION

We consider sushi an art form,and our chefs meticulously craft it. Using the freshest ingredients, they create visually stunning pieces that tantalize the palate and captivate the senses. This commitment to artistry ensures a unique sushi experience with every visit.

CORE VALUES

Commiment in freshness, locally sourced and in-season ingredients are our goals.

Our philosophy centers on genuine hospitality, ensuring each guest feels embraced as an esteemed member of our extended family. This highlights both the welcoming nature and the personalized service you strive for.
